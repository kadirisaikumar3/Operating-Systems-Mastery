# 11-FAQs
# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

## What is a Thread?

Smallest unit of execution.

---

## Why are Threads used?

To improve performance and responsiveness.

---

## What is Multithreading?

Running multiple Threads concurrently.

---

## Do Threads share memory?

Yes.

Threads share Process memory.

---

## What is TCB?

Thread Control Block.

---

## What is TID?

Thread Identifier.

---

## Why are Threads faster?

Because they share resources.

---

## What are User Threads?

Threads managed by User Library.

---

## What are Kernel Threads?

Threads managed by Operating System.

---

## Can a Process exist without Threads?

Every Process has at least one Thread.
