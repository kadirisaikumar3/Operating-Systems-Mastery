# 10-Common-Mistakes
# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Thinking Virtual Memory increases RAM.

Wrong.

It creates the illusion of larger memory.

---

## Mistake 2

Confusing Pages and Frames.

Pages

Logical Memory.

Frames

Physical Memory.

---

## Mistake 3

Ignoring Page Fault overhead.

Disk access is much slower than RAM.

---

## Mistake 4

Thinking FIFO is always efficient.

FIFO may suffer from Belady's Anomaly.

---

## Mistake 5

Ignoring Thrashing.

Frequent Page Faults reduce CPU utilization.

---
