# 10-Common-Mistakes
# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Confusing Logical and Physical Addresses.

Logical Address

Generated by CPU.

Physical Address

Generated by MMU.

---

## Mistake 2

Thinking Internal and External Fragmentation are identical.

Wrong.

Internal

Unused allocated memory.

External

Separated free memory.

---

## Mistake 3

Ignoring MMU.

MMU performs address translation.

---

## Mistake 4

Believing Best Fit is always best.

It minimizes waste but may increase fragmentation.

---

## Mistake 5

Ignoring Compaction.

Compaction reduces External Fragmentation.

---

## Interview Advice

Memory

↓

Address

↓

MMU

↓

Allocation

↓

Fragmentation

↓

Compaction
