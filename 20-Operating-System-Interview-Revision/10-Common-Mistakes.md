# 10-Common-Mistakes
# ❌ Common Interview Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Memorizing definitions only.

Understand concepts deeply.

---

## Mistake 2

Ignoring diagrams.

Interviewers love diagrams.

---

## Mistake 3

Not learning comparisons.

Most interviews ask comparisons.

---

## Mistake 4

No real-world examples.

Always connect concepts to real systems.

---

## Mistake 5

Skipping Linux concepts.

Linux is frequently asked.

---

## Mistake 6

Not practicing explanations aloud.

Communication matters.
