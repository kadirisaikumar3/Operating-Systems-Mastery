# 10-Common-Mistakes
# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Confusing Pages with Frames.

Page

Logical Memory

Frame

Physical Memory

---

## Mistake 2

Thinking Paging removes all fragmentation.

Wrong.

Paging removes External Fragmentation but may cause Internal Fragmentation.

---

## Mistake 3

Ignoring Page Table.

Every logical address translation requires a Page Table lookup.

---

## Mistake 4

Confusing Paging and Segmentation.

Paging

Physical Memory Management

Segmentation

Logical Program Organization

---

## Mistake 5

Ignoring TLB.

TLB improves address translation speed.

---
