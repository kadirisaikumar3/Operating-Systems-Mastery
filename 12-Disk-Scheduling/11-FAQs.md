# 11-FAQs
# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Disk Scheduling?

A method used to decide the order of servicing disk I/O requests.

---

## Why is Disk Scheduling needed?

To reduce seek time and improve performance.

---

## What is Seek Time?

Time required to move disk head to the desired track.

---

## Which algorithm is simplest?

FCFS.

---

## Which algorithm may cause starvation?

SSTF.

---

## What is Elevator Algorithm?

SCAN.

---

## Which is better: HDD or SSD?

SSD is faster because it has no moving parts.

---

## What is RAID?

A technique using multiple disks for performance and reliability.

---

## Where is Disk Scheduling used?

- Operating Systems
- Databases
- Cloud Servers
- Storage Systems
