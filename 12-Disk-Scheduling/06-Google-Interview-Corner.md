# 06-Google-Interview-Corner
# 🚀 Google Interview Corner

Disk Scheduling is a frequently asked Operating Systems topic.

---

# Question 1

What is Disk Scheduling?

Expected Answer

Disk Scheduling determines the order in which disk I/O requests are serviced to minimize access time.

---

# Question 2

FCFS vs SSTF

| FCFS | SSTF |
|-------|------|
| Simple | Better Performance |
| Fair | May Cause Starvation |
| High Seek Time | Lower Seek Time |

---

# Question 3

SCAN vs C-SCAN

| SCAN | C-SCAN |
|--------|--------|
| Moves Both Directions | One Direction |
| Variable Wait Time | Uniform Wait Time |

---

# Question 4

LOOK vs SCAN

Expected Answer

LOOK only travels to the last request.

SCAN travels to the disk end.

LOOK is more efficient.

---

# Question 5

What is RAID?

Expected Answer

RAID combines multiple disks to improve performance and reliability.
