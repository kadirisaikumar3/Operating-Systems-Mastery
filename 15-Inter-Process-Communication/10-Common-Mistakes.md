# 10-Common-Mistakes
# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Thinking IPC is only for networking.

Wrong.

IPC also works between local processes.

---

## Mistake 2

Ignoring Synchronization.

Shared Memory requires synchronization.

---

## Mistake 3

Confusing Pipe and Socket.

Pipe

Local

Socket

Local + Network

---

## Mistake 4

Thinking Semaphores transfer data.

Wrong.

Semaphores synchronize processes.

---

## Mistake 5

Ignoring Race Conditions.

Shared resources require protection.

---
