# 📚 IPC Concepts

## What is IPC?

IPC (Inter Process Communication) is a mechanism that allows processes to communicate and exchange data.

---

## Why IPC is Needed?

Processes run independently.

Without IPC:

- No communication
- No data sharing
- No synchronization

---

## Basic IPC Flow

Process A

↓

IPC Mechanism

↓

Process B

---

## Goals

- Communication
- Synchronization
- Resource Sharing
- Coordination

---

## Types of IPC

### Shared Memory

Processes share a memory region.

### Message Passing

Processes exchange messages.
