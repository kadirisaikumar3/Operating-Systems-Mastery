# 11-FAQs
# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

## What is IPC?

A mechanism that allows processes to communicate.

---

## Why is IPC needed?

For communication, synchronization, and data sharing.

---

## Fastest IPC mechanism?

Shared Memory.

---

## Which IPC supports networking?

Sockets.

---

## What is a Pipe?

A communication channel between related processes.

---

## What is a Semaphore?

A synchronization mechanism.

---

## What is a Signal?

A notification mechanism.

---

## Where is IPC used?

- Browsers
- Databases
- Cloud Systems
- Operating Systems
- Networking
