# 11-FAQs
# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

## What is a Process?

A program in execution.

---

## What is PCB?

Process Control Block.

Stores process information.

---

## What is PID?

Unique Process Identifier.

---

## Why is PCB needed?

To manage and track processes.

---

## What is Context Switching?

Switching CPU from one process to another.

---

## Why is Context Switching slow?

Because process information must be saved and restored.

---

## What is Process Creation?

Creating a new running process.

---

## What is Process Termination?

Ending execution of a process.

---

## Does every Process have its own memory?

Yes.

Each process has its own address space.

---

## Why is Chrome multi-process?

For

- Stability
- Security
- Better Performance
