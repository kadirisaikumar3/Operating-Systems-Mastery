# 10-Common-Mistakes
# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Thinking Program and Process are the same.

Wrong.

Program is stored on disk.

Process is running.

---

## Mistake 2

Confusing Process with Thread.

Wrong.

A Process can contain multiple Threads.

---

## Mistake 3

Thinking Context Switching is free.

Wrong.

It introduces CPU overhead.

---

## Mistake 4

Ignoring PCB.

PCB is one of the most important Operating System concepts.

---

## Mistake 5

Thinking all Chrome tabs use one process.

Modern Chrome uses multiple processes.

---

## Interview Advice

Explain

Program

↓

Process

↓

PCB

↓

Context Switching

This order gives a complete answer.
