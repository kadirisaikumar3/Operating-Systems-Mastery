# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Operating System?

Operating System is system software that manages hardware resources.

---

## Why is Operating System required?

To provide interface between User and Hardware.

---

## What are examples?

Windows

Linux

macOS

Android

iOS

---

## Can Computer work without Operating System?

Technically Yes.

Practically No.

General users cannot use the computer effectively without an OS.

---

## What is Kernel?

Core part of Operating System.

---

## What is Shell?

User Interface of Operating System.

---

## What is Process?

Program in Execution.

---

## What is Thread?

Smallest unit of execution inside Process.

---

## What is Booting?

Loading Operating System into Memory.

---

## Difference between Program and Process?
