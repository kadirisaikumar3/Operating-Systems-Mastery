# 06-Google-Interview-Corner
# 🚀 Google Interview Corner

CPU Scheduling is one of the most frequently discussed Operating System topics in technical interviews.

---

# Question 1

What is CPU Scheduling?

Expected Answer

CPU Scheduling is the process of selecting the next process from the Ready Queue for CPU execution.

---

# Question 2

FCFS vs SJF

| FCFS | SJF |
|------|-----|
| Arrival Order | Shortest Burst First |
| Simple | Better Performance |
| Convoy Effect | Starvation Possible |

---

# Question 3

Why is SJF optimal?

Expected Answer

SJF minimizes the average waiting time when burst times are known.

---

# Question 4

Why is Round Robin suitable for Time-Sharing Systems?

Expected Points

- Fairness
- Responsiveness
- Equal CPU Allocation
- Reduced Starvation

---

# Question 5

Explain the Convoy Effect.

Expected Answer

In FCFS, a long process delays shorter processes, increasing waiting time.

---

# Question 6

Difference between Scheduler and Dispatcher.

| Scheduler | Dispatcher |
|-----------|------------|
| Chooses Process | Gives CPU Control |
| Decision Maker | Executes Decision |
