## Scheduling Criteria

- CPU Utilization
- Throughput
- Waiting Time
- Turnaround Time
- Response Time

---

## Formulas

Turnaround Time

TAT = Completion Time − Arrival Time

---

Waiting Time

WT = TAT − Burst Time

---

Response Time

RT = First CPU Allocation − Arrival Time
