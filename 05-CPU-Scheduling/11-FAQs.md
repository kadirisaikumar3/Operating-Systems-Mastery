# 11-FAQs
# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

## What is CPU Scheduling?

Selecting the next process for CPU execution.

---

## Why is Scheduling required?

To improve CPU utilization and reduce waiting time.

---

## What is FCFS?

First Come First Serve Scheduling.

---

## What is SJF?

Shortest Job First Scheduling.

---

## What is SRTF?

Shortest Remaining Time First.

---

## What is Round Robin?

Preemptive scheduling using a Time Quantum.

---

## What is Starvation?

A process waits indefinitely because higher-priority processes keep executing.

---

## What is Aging?

Increasing the priority of waiting processes to prevent starvation.

---

## Which algorithm is best?

There is no universal best algorithm.

The choice depends on:

- System requirements
- Workload
- Response time
- Fairness
