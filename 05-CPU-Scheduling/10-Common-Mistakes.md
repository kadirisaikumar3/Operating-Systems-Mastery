# 10-Common-Mistakes
# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Confusing Waiting Time and Turnaround Time.

Remember

Waiting Time excludes execution time.

---

## Mistake 2

Forgetting Arrival Time while solving numerical problems.

Always consider Arrival Time unless stated otherwise.

---

## Mistake 3

Drawing incorrect Gantt Charts.

Always arrange processes according to the scheduling algorithm.

---

## Mistake 4

Ignoring Time Quantum in Round Robin.

Without Time Quantum, Round Robin cannot work.

---

## Mistake 5

Thinking SJF always works best.

It requires knowledge of Burst Time.

---

## Interview Advice

Always:

Draw Gantt Chart

↓

Calculate Completion Time

↓

Calculate TAT

↓

Calculate WT

↓

Calculate RT
