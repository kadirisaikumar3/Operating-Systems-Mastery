# 11-FAQs
# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

## What is a System Call?

A mechanism used by applications to request OS services.

---

## Why are System Calls needed?

To safely access system resources.

---

## What is Kernel Mode?

Privileged execution mode with full access.

---

## What is User Mode?

Restricted execution mode for applications.

---

## What does fork() do?

Creates a child process.

---

## What does exec() do?

Loads a new program into a process.

---

## What does wait() do?

Waits for a child process to finish.

---

## What is socket()?

A communication endpoint used in networking.

---

## Where are System Calls used?

- Browsers
- Databases
- Cloud Systems
- Mobile Apps
- Operating Systems
