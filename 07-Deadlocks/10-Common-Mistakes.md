# 10-Common-Mistakes
# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Thinking Unsafe State always means Deadlock.

Wrong.

Unsafe State may lead to Deadlock but is not Deadlock itself.

---

## Mistake 2

Confusing Deadlock with Starvation.

Deadlock

Processes wait forever.

Starvation

One process waits indefinitely while others continue.

---

## Mistake 3

Ignoring all Coffman Conditions.

All four conditions are necessary.

---

## Mistake 4

Thinking Banker's Algorithm detects Deadlocks.

Wrong.

It avoids Deadlocks.

---

## Mistake 5

Ignoring Resource Allocation Graph.

Always draw RAG before solving.

---
