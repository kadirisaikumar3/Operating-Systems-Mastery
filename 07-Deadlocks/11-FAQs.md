# 11-FAQs
# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Deadlock?

Processes wait forever for each other's resources.

---

## Why does Deadlock occur?

Because all four Coffman Conditions hold.

---

## What are Coffman Conditions?

- Mutual Exclusion
- Hold and Wait
- No Preemption
- Circular Wait

---

## What is Safe State?

A state where every process can complete.

---

## What is Unsafe State?

A state that may eventually lead to Deadlock.

---

## Which algorithm avoids Deadlock?

Banker's Algorithm.

---

## Which graph represents Deadlocks?

Resource Allocation Graph.

---

## Can Java programs Deadlock?

Yes.

Improper lock ordering may create Deadlocks.

---

## How do we prevent Deadlocks?

Break any one Coffman Condition.
