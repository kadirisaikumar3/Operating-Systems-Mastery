# 11-FAQs
# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Process Synchronization?

It coordinates concurrent execution to protect shared resources.

---

## Why is Synchronization required?

To avoid Race Conditions and inconsistent data.

---

## What is a Critical Section?

A section of code that accesses shared resources.

---

## What is a Race Condition?

When multiple processes modify shared data simultaneously.

---

## What is Mutual Exclusion?

Only one process can enter the Critical Section at a time.

---

## What is Semaphore?

A synchronization mechanism that controls access to shared resources.

---

## What is Mutex?

A lock allowing only one thread into the Critical Section.

---

## What is a Monitor?

A high-level synchronization construct.

---

## Which Java keyword is used?

synchronized
