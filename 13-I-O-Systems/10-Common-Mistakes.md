# ❌ Common Mistakes

## Mistake 1

Confusing Device Driver and Device Controller.

Driver

Software

Controller

Hardware

---

## Mistake 2

Thinking DMA eliminates CPU completely.

Wrong.

CPU initiates DMA transfer.

---

## Mistake 3

Ignoring Interrupt Overhead.

Interrupt handling consumes CPU resources.

---

## Mistake 4

Confusing Buffering and Spooling.

Buffering

Temporary memory storage.

Spooling

Queue-based device management.

---

## Mistake 5

Thinking Programmed I/O is efficient.

It wastes CPU cycles.
